David Cherkasskiy

Notable Ukrainian animator, director and screenwriter

Biography

Born in 1932 in Shpola, Ukrainian SSR.
After graduating from the Kyiv Institute of Civil Engineering, he worked for a while, but David wanted to work in animation since childhood, so in 1959 he came to work at the "Kyivnaukfilm" studio as an animator.
Since 1964, he has been the director-producer of "Kyivnaukfilm"

In 1991, became the President of the International Festival of Animated Films "KROK".
Member of the National Union of Cinematographers and Journalists of Ukraine
He died on October 30, 2018 at the age of 87


Cherkaskyi himself works in the drawn technology. In his films, Cherkassky used several innovative techniques for Soviet cinematography. He combined animation and game shooting in the frame. Made three-dimensional "flyovers" that imitate panning in art cinema, creating such an effect as if the camera were flying around the characters. In his films, sounds like "Boom", "Bang", etc. were drawn, just like in comics.
As the animator himself said, he did not attend art schools, but learned to draw on his own.
